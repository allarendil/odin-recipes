Let's make Dinner!

One of my favorite weekday dinners is a simple sausage sheet pan.

Not only do the kids love this meal,
but it's super easy to whip up
and is a great way to use up any veggies in your fridge!

I tend to use the same veggies based on what my kids like
but you can use anything you wish!
Same goes for the spices,
feel free to experiment!

I like to serve this with some rice that I just
throw into my rice cooker and top with soy and hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Smoked Sausage
  • 1 Package Brussel Sprouts
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 tbs Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 3 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Wash and chop your produce and sausage into bite size pieces
  3. Add everything right onto your parchment lined pan
  4. Coat veggies with olive oil
  5. Add the spices
  6. Mix everything together thoroughly
  7. Spread into even layer
  8. bake about 25 to 30 minutes

And that's it! My kids love this, and its a great way
to get them to eat brussel sprouts as they soak up the juices
from the meat, making them extra delicious!
