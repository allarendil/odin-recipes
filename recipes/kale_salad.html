Let's Make a Side!

This kale recipe is super simple, and the kids love it!
It takes only minutes to prepare, no cooking required!

The lemon juice added to the kale, and the kneading from your hands
tenderize the kale, making it perfectly to eat raw.
I make this every week!

My family likes the tartness of the lemon juice added,
it makes it taste sweet and sour
But that may not be for you,
so you can add less lemon juice and a little more oil!
Give it a try!

This recipe will yield about 4 cups of kale, depending on the
size of the bunch. Keep in mind it will wilt as you kneed it

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of kale
  • Juice from 1/2 a lemon
  • 2 tbs Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Thoroughly wash the kale
  2. Pull the kale off the stem and rip into bite size pieces
  3. Add all ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Kneed everything together thoroughly, scrunching the kale with your hands for several minutes

And that's it! You will see the kale wilt as you kneed it
just give it a taste to check for desired texture and it's ready to eat!
