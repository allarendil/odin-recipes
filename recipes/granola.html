Let's make a Snack!

This granola dish is easy to whip up in a matter of minutes!
Prep it ahead of time and store in an airtight container for
an easy snack!

This healthy snack will last a couple weeks if stored properly,
but it doesn't last that long in my house!
My favorite way to eat this is in a bowl with some milk
like an almost guilt-free cereal!

The ingredients listed below are very loose
you can modify, omit, or add onto any of them! Such as using
honey instead of maple syrup, or any kind of nuts you have on hand.
Make it nut-free and just use oats and dried fruit!
The world is your oyster!

This recipe will yield about 4 cups of granola, but that depends on
how much you add! I prefer to measure with the heart and just throw
everything in.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup Nuts of choice (I like to use a mix)
  • 1 cup Dry Oats
  • 1 cup Chocolate Chips
  • 3 tbs Flax Seed
  • 3 tbs Chia Seed
  • 1/2 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 cup Melted Coconut Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Dump all ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Mix everything together thoroughly
  4. Spread mixture onto a parchment lined baking sheet
  5. bake about 25 minutes

And that's it! Let it cool and store in a cool dry space.
But don't forget to enjoy some while its still warm!
